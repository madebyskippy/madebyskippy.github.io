Circle Cycle - 2018



Control the circles of a spirograph to create patterns, shapes, and hit the stars.

Made with 3 pressure sensors so multiple people can control multiple circles at once.

Created for the class Designing for Kids, and shown at the New York Hall of Science in 2018. It was also shown at Come Out and Play After Dark 2018.
