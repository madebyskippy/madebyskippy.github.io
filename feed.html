FEED - 2017



Fish Elder's Eternal Digestion (FEED) is a 3v3 character-based multiplayer game where basketball meets tug-o-war meets three-person potato sack race in a bizarre underwater world of sentient limbs and insatiable Fish Elders. In FEED, swimming together means winning together.

The game was shown at Indiecade as part of the official selection.

The game is now released here!

Worked in a team of 6 as project manager, as well as assisting with code and animation.
