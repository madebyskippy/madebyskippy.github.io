Magic Book - 2019



At Potion, a projection mapping project I worked on was a magic book. Each page has unique dynamic content that brings the book alive. I built a system to read the sensors and play video, as well as be flexible for changing the video and audio content so that the visual designers could test different layouts and animations. I also oversaw the AV and electronics setup for the installation.
