Spring Sprong - 2017



Spring Sprong is an alternative controller game inspired by the Ping Pong movie.
Hit the ball when the light is on your side. Time your swing for different shots. Rally for long enough and the shot speed will adjust into a joyful equilibrium!

The game was shown at Come Out and Play After Dark 2017 and After Dark 2019.

Worked with Laurenz and Milan. I took care of systems code, base gameplay code, and electronics.
